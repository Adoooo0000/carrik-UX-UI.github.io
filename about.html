About Me

  Hi there! My name is Carrik, and I am a UX/UI designer with a passion for innovation and learning. I received my training under the Google UX Design Certificate Course, which gave me a strong foundation in the design process, from research to usability testing. Since then, I have worked on two projects on my own, allowing me to gain hands-on experience and refine my skills. In addition to my design expertise, I have a basic knowledge of HTML and CSS, C++, Excel VBA, and Python. I am proficient in using Figma and Adobe XD, and I am always eager to learn new tools. In fact, this website was created with the help of AI code editor tools. As a designer, I am constantly inspired by the work of others and regularly watch other designers’ projects to stay up-to-date with the latest design trends. I am excited to continue my journey as a UX/UI designer and create innovative solutions that make a positive impact.
